Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how O Santo Terço ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in our prayer application. We prioritize your privacy and aim to be transparent about the technical permissions required for the app to function.

1. Information Collection and Use

While the app is designed for prayer and does not require a user account, the following technical data may be processed to provide app functionality:

  • Advertising Identifier (AD_ID): We use the Google Advertising ID to provide advertisements. This identifier does not reveal your name or email but allows for basic ad-serving functionality.
  • Device State (READ_PHONE_STATE): This is used solely to detect incoming calls. This allows the app to automatically pause the prayer audio so you can answer your phone, and resume once the call ends. We do not access your call logs or contacts.

2. Technical Permissions

To provide a seamless experience, the app requests the following system permissions:

  • Internet & Network State: Used to stream prayer audios (if applicable) and check for a valid connection before attempting to play media.
  • Notifications & Alarms: Used to send prayer reminders and ensure scheduled prayer alerts trigger at the exact time you've requested (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM).
  • Boot Completed: Allows the app to reschedule your set prayer reminders if you restart your device.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Data related to the Advertising ID is handled by Google Play Services in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy. No other data is transmitted to external servers.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we may take necessary actions.
